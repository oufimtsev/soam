Welcome

A product (project or program) succeeds when it satisfies the objectives of its stakeholders. Understanding who stakeholders are and what their objectives are is the bedrock on which a successful product is built. Our Stakeholder & Objective Analysis and Management (SOAM) tool makes it faster and easier for users to:
  • Identify, understand, and document:
    • Stakeholders
    • Stakeholder objectives,
  • Find and resolve conflicts between them,
  • Develop a communications plan, and
  • Engage stakeholders.
